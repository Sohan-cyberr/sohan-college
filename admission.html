Eligibility Criteria

Candidates seeking admission must ensure they fulfill the eligibility conditions prescribed by the affiliated university and regulatory authorities.

Undergraduate Programs (UG)

  • Passed 10+2 (HSC or equivalent) from a recognized board
  • Commerce stream required for B.Com programs
  • Science stream preferred for B.Sc programs
  • Minimum qualifying marks as per university norms

Postgraduate Programs (PG)

  • Bachelor’s degree in relevant discipline from a recognized university
  • Minimum 45%–50% aggregate marks (relaxation as per rules)
  • Final year students may apply provisionally

Note: Eligibility criteria may vary based on government or university notifications.

Application Process

The admission process is conducted in a transparent and merit-based manner. Applicants are advised to read all instructions carefully before applying.

  1. Register on the college admission portal
  2. Fill in personal, academic, and contact details
  3. Upload scanned copies of required documents
  4. Pay the application fee online
  5. Submit the application form
  6. Track application status using login credentials

Shortlisted candidates will be informed via email/SMS regarding further admission steps.

Documents Required

  • 10th & 12th Mark Sheets
  • Leaving Certificate / Transfer Certificate
  • Caste Certificate (if applicable)
  • Aadhar Card / Government ID
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Graduation Mark Sheets (for PG admission)
